Summary

This training unit is related to the Humain Sciences Center (in French Centre de Sciences Humaines) of INSA Toulouse. During this training unit, i have the chance to develop my team management and humain skill, from creativity method to moral principles that apply to the practice of engineering.

Innovation and Humanity

Languages, Human and Social Sciences are very important for an engineer in this modern world. The main objective of Humain Sciences Center of INSA is to prepare students to integrate the professional world and to put their actions in perspective, taking into account the social, economic, ethical and cultural context that surrounds them. It is also to get them to communicate effectively with their French and foreign interlocutors. The teaching team also works in close collaboration with teachers in scientific and technical disciplines and with inviters from the professional world. It also supports students with innovative projects or wishing to diversify their course.

Social Psychology

During this course, i have the change to discuss about how people's thoughts, feelings, and behaviors are influenced by the actual, imagined, or implied presence of others. It is an exciting topic because it is so familiar and relevant to our day-to-day lives.

Begin with an analyze of my favorite movie: "12 Angry Men" where 12 men debate the fate of a young boy charged with murdering his father. This is a movie where tension comes from personality conflict, dialogue and body language and not action, where logic, emotion and prejudice struggle to control the field. The principle of reasonable doubt, the belief that a defendant is innocent until proven guilty, is one of the most enlightened elements of this movie.

The next part of this course is an oral presentation. My team and were able to talk about "Intercultural Communications". Intercultural communication requires an understanding that different cultures have different customs, standards, social and even thought patterns. Intercultural communication skills are those required to communicate, or share information, with people from other cultures and social groups. While language skills may be an important part of intercultural communication, they are by no means the only requirement. For me, a good intercultural communication skills requires a willingness to accept differences and adapt to them. You can find my team's presentation here.
