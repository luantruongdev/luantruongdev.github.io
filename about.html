About me

I'm an engineering student in final year (Bac+5) at INSA Toulouse. My background is Embedded System Engineering, specialized in Innovative Smart System.

I entered INSA Toulouse in its 1st general year. And in my 2nd and 3rd year, i chose the IMACS (Materials, Components and Systems Engineering) speciality.

In my 4th year at INSA, i chose to join Computer Science And Electrical Engineering Department, specialize in Embedded Systems as i've always had a huge passion about this field.

And in final year at INSA, i joined the Innovative Smart System speciality, where i learned about the concept of DevOps, Cloud Computing, Smart IoT Object...

Professionally, I was able to complete two internships during my INSA formation. The first one is in the automotive sector, ACTIA Group in Toulouse, where i had my first experience with working in a professinal environment. The second one in more oriented to technical environment, my company was Lightricity in Oxford, the UK, where my main task is to develop firmware for an ultra-low power sensing IoT platform of Silicon Labs using BLE (Bluetooth Low Energy).

About the Innovative Smart System formation at INSA Toulouse

The Multidisciplinary Transversal Course (PTP) Innovative Smart System (ISS) aims to train students in giving them the capabitity of innovating, designing, producing, distributing and promoting a "smart system", from the development of components to market analysis, while taking into account environmental and societal aspects.

This formation is formulated with many potential field in our modern world, like: The Internet of Things, Cloud Computing, Big Data, Smart Connected Object as well as computer network, electronic and software development. Through this formation, I was therefore able to strengthen my technical knowledge in IoT as well as project management and organization skill.
